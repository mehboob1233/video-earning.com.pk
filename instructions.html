Withdrawal Form Instructions

Welcome to the Withdrawal Form! Follow these instructions to complete the process:

  • Fill in your Name.
  • Enter your Phone Number. It should be a 10-digit number.
  • Choose your preferred Withdrawal Option from the dropdown (EasyPaisa or JazzCash).
  • Specify the amount of Required Money.
  • Add an optional Message for any additional information.
  • Click the Submit button to process your withdrawal.

If you have any questions or issues, feel free to contact our support team at support@example.com.

Thank you for using our service!
